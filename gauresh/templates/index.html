Four steps in farming

Preparation

"This involves preparing the land for planting by clearing it of any debris or unwanted vegetation, tilling or plowing the soil, and adding any necessary fertilizers or soil amendments to improve the soil's quality.

Planting

Once the land has been prepared, the next step is to plant the seeds or seedlings. This can involve a range of techniques depending on the crop being grown and the farming method being used.

Maintenance

Once the crops are planted, they require regular care and maintenance. This can involve activities such as watering, weeding, pest control, and monitoring for diseases or other issues that could impact the health and growth of the crops.

Harvesting

Finally, once the crops have reached maturity and are ready to be harvested, they can be harvested by hand or using machines such as combines or harvesters.
